Tigers eat prey that range from the size of termites to the baby elephants. However for them to be healthy, they need to prey weighing about 45 pounds (deer, moose, cows, horse, etc). To find their prey, they mainly rely on sight. They stalk the prey and slowly inch closer to it and when they are close enough, they jump and bite the neck. More Info

Malala Yousafzai

"Pakistani Activist"

Malala Yousafzai's city of Mingora was invaded by the Taliban.
They were "enforcing" the laws of Shariah, which meant that women were not allowed to go to school and other extremeties.
However, this group took it to the extreme with bomb attacks.
Yousafzai was once on her way to school, when they bombed her bus.
She was badly injured, but she didn't give up her fighting spirit.
She worked to fight for women.
She managed to go to therapy for healing.
This process eventually reached her to gain a nobel peace prize.

A quote by Yousafzai that I personally found super inspiring is...

"If one man can destroy everything, why can't one girl change it?"
